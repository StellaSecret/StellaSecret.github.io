StrategicJournal

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Summary: Strategic Journal does not collect any data on our servers. Your journal entries are stored locally on your device and, if enabled, synchronized to your own personal Google Drive. We have no access to any of your data.

1. Who we are

Strategic Journal is a mobile decision journal and prediction application, developed privately by StellaSecret. It is not operated by a commercial entity. App Identifier: com.strategicjournal.

2. Data Collection

Strategic Journal does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. No user account is required. No registration is requested.

✕ No identification data (name, email, phone)
✕ No location data
✕ No navigation or usage data
✕ No advertising identifier
✕ No biometric or health data

The journal entries you write (hypotheses, decisions, predictions) are stored only on your device, in a local database encrypted by Android. They never leave your phone unless you explicitly enable Google Drive synchronization.

3. Google Drive Sync (optional)

If you choose to connect your Google account, the app uses the Google Drive API to synchronize your entries into a private storage space called App Data Folder. This folder:

  • Is accessible only by you and the Strategic Journal app
  • Is not visible in your regular Google Drive
  • Contains only the JSON files of your journal entries
  • Is automatically deleted if you uninstall the application

We have no access to any of these files. They are stored directly in your Google account, under your exclusive control.

4. Third-party Services

The application uses the following services:

  • Google Drive API — optional data synchronization (Google privacy policy)
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication for Drive sync

These services are subject to Google's privacy policies. Outside of these services (if sync is enabled), no data passes through a third-party server.

5. Data Storage

✓ All entries are stored locally on your device (encrypted Room database)
✓ Drive synchronization is entirely optional and revocable at any time
✓ No remote server of ours receives your data
✓ Uninstalling the app erases all local data

6. Advertising and Analytics

Strategic Journal contains no advertising and integrates no analysis or tracking tools.

7. Android Permissions

  • INTERNET — required for Google Drive synchronization (if enabled)
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — checks network availability before attempting synchronization

No location, camera, microphone, or contact permissions are required.
