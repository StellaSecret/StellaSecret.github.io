TripMind

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Summary: TripMind does not collect any personal data. The application operates entirely using free public APIs, with no user account, no tokens, no server, and no tracking.

1. Who we are

TripMind is a mobile travel assistant application for France, developed privately by StellaSecret. It is not operated by a commercial entity. App Identifier: com.stellasecret.tripmind.

2. Data Collection

TripMind does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No user account is required. No registration is requested.

✕ No identification data (name, email, phone)
✕ No stored or transmitted location history
✕ No navigation or usage data
✕ No advertising identifier
✕ No biometric or health data

Device geolocation, if authorized, is used strictly in real-time to calculate weather information, air quality, and routes. it is never recorded or transmitted to our servers (we do not have any).

3. Third-party Services

TripMind relies exclusively on public and free APIs. Your requests transit directly from your device to these services — no proxy or intermediate server is used.

  • Open-Meteo — weather and UV index (terms of use)
  • Open-Meteo / Copernicus CAMS — air quality and pollen
  • Base Adresse Nationale — French address geocoding
  • OSRM / OpenStreetMap — road route calculations
  • Transitous / MOTIS 2 — train and public transport schedules (transitous.org)

4. Data Storage

The only data stored locally on your device are display preferences (e.g., light/dark theme, language) and recent search cache. This data never leaves your phone.

✓ No remote server receives your data.
✓ No remote database is associated with the app.
✓ Uninstalling the app deletes all local data.

5. Advertising and Analytics

TripMind contains no advertising and does not integrate any analysis or tracking tools (no Firebase Analytics, no Crashlytics, no third-party SDKs).

6. Android Permissions

  • INTERNET — required to query weather, air quality, geocoding, and transport APIs.
  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION — optional, only used if you ask the app to detect your position automatically. Position is never stored.
