GameTracker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Summary: GameTracker does not collect any personal data. All your game data is stored locally on your device. Google Drive synchronization and real-time groups are optional features that you control entirely.

1. Who we are

GameTracker is a mobile score tracking application for board games, developed privately by StellaSecret. It is not operated by a commercial entity. App Identifier: com.example.game_tracker.

2. Data Collection

GameTracker does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We have no servers of our own.

✕ No identification data (name, email, phone)
✕ No location data
✕ No navigation or usage data
✕ No advertising identifier
✕ No biometric or health data

The only data created by the app are the games, players, and matches you enter yourself. This data stays on your device, unless you explicitly enable Google Drive sync or groups (see sections 3 and 4).

3. Google Drive Sync (optional)

GameTracker offers a manual backup feature to your personal Google Drive. This feature is entirely optional and requires your explicit authorization.

  • Data is stored in the application's private folder on your personal Google Drive (appDataFolder), invisible to the standard Drive interface.
  • Only you can access this folder. We do not have access to it.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers during this synchronization.
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google account settings.

4. Real-time Groups — Firebase (optional)

Premium subscribers can create real-time synchronized player groups via Google Firebase / Cloud Firestore. This feature is entirely optional and requires explicit Google sign-in.

  • Group data (games, players, scores) is stored in Firestore, accessible only to group members you invite via email.
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication via your Google account.
  • You can leave a group or delete your data at any time from within the app.

5. In-app Purchases — RevenueCat (optional)

Premium subscriptions are managed via RevenueCat, a third-party service. RevenueCat may collect an anonymous purchase identifier and transaction data according to their privacy policy.

6. Local Storage

If synchronization is not enabled, all your data is stored only in your device's local memory via SharedPreferences.

✓ No remote server receives your data by default.
✓ Uninstalling the app deletes all local data.
✓ No data is shared between users without your explicit consent.

7. Advertising and Analytics

GameTracker contains no advertising and integrates no analysis or tracking tools.
