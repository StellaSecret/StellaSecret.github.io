Smart Shopping Calculator

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Summary: Smart Shopping Calculator does not collect any personal data. All your calculations and comparisons are performed locally on your device. The app communicates with no external server, except for the optional Open Food Facts lookup triggered when you scan a barcode.

1. Who we are

Smart Shopping Calculator is an Android app for comparing unit prices (toilet paper, protein powder), developed privately by StellaSecret. It is not operated by a commercial entity. App identifier: com.stellasecret.smartshoppingcalculator.

2. Data Collection

Smart Shopping Calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. We have no servers of our own.

✕ No identification data (name, email, phone)
✕ No location data
✕ No navigation or usage data
✕ No advertising identifier
✕ No biometric or health data

The only data created by the app is the prices and product details you enter yourself. This data is stored locally in your browser (via localStorage) if you use the session save feature. It never leaves your device.

3. Barcode Scanning — Open Food Facts (optional)

Smart Shopping Calculator offers a barcode scanning feature to auto-fill fields for a protein powder product. This feature is entirely optional and is only triggered by your explicit action.

  • Barcode recognition is performed entirely on your device using ML Kit (Google) — no image is sent to any external server.
  • Once a barcode is detected, the app queries the public Open Food Facts API to retrieve the product's nutritional information (name, weight, protein content).
  • Only the raw barcode number (e.g. 3270160513847) is sent to Open Food Facts — no identifying data about you is transmitted.
  • Open Food Facts is a non-profit project. See their privacy policy for their own data practices.
  • If the product is not found or there is no network, no data is transmitted and you can enter product details manually.

ℹ This feature is available in the Android app only, not in the web version.

4. Camera

The app requests camera access only when you trigger a barcode scan. The camera permission is used solely to capture a photo of the product to scan. No image is retained, transmitted, or analysed beyond reading the barcode.

✓ Camera permission is never requested at launch.
✓ No image is stored or sent to any server.
✓ You can deny the permission — the app remains fully functional via manual entry.

5. Local Storage

If you use the "Save session" feature, your comparison data (prices, product details) is saved in your browser's local storage via localStorage.

✓ This data stays on your device and is never transmitted.
✓ You can delete it at any time using the "Clear session" button in the app.
✓ Uninstalling the app deletes all local data.

6. Advertising and Analytics

Smart Shopping Calculator contains no advertising and integrates no analytics or tracking tools (no Firebase Analytics, no Crashlytics, no advertising SDK, no third-party cookies).

7. Android Permissions Used

  • CAMERA — used only during a barcode scan, on your explicit action.
  • INTERNET — used only to query Open Food Facts during a barcode scan, on your explicit action.

The app requests no other permissions (no access to contacts, location, external storage, microphone, etc.).

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

Since Smart Shopping Calculator processes no personal data on its own servers, your rights of access, rectification, and deletion are naturally upheld: your data is on your device, under your full control.

For any privacy-related question, you can reach us at the address listed in section 10.

9. Children

Smart Shopping Calculator is not specifically directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the date at the top of this page will be changed accordingly. Significant changes will be noted in the app's release notes on the Google Play Store.
